What are you going to learn?

Camera Settings and Functions

Understanding the different settings and functions of your camera, such as shooting modes, exposure compensation, white balance, image quality, and file formats, exploring the various buttons, dials, and menus on your camera and their purpose.

Framing & Composition

Learning the rule of thirds and how to use it to create balanced and visually appealing compositions and different composition techniques like leading lines, framing, symmetry, and the use of negative space to understand how to position and frame your subject within the frame to create more impactful images

Exposure Triangle

Understanding the 3 essential elements of exposure: shutter speed, aperture, and ISO and balancing them to achieve proper exposure and desired effects. Also, explore the relationship between these elements and how they affect the overall look of your photos

Lighting types and techniques

Explore the qualities of natural light, including direction, intensity, and color temperature and exploring different lighting techniques, such as using backlighting, sidelighting, and diffused light

White Balance

Understanding white balance and adjusting it to ensure accurate color reproduction under different lighting conditions.

Utilising Focus and Depth of Field

Mastering autofocus modes and techniques to ensure accurate and sharp focus and also learn how to creatively use shallow depth of field or deep depth of field to enhance your photographs.

Choosing the right lenses

Learning the different lens types and their characteristics, such as wide-angle, standard, telephoto, and macro lenses and also choosing the right lens for specific types of photography, such as portraits, landscapes, or close-ups.
